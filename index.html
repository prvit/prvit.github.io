About

A bit about me

I'm a software engineer with over 10 years of experience designing and building robust backend systems. I specialize in the .NET ecosystem — C#, ASP.NET Core, and Azure — with a strong focus on distributed architectures, API design, and high-throughput transactional systems.

At PAR Technology, I maintain and evolve mission-critical infrastructure for a large-scale retail fuel network, handling customer accounts, loyalty programs, and payment flows across distributed services. I care deeply about system reliability, performance, and clean, maintainable code.

Outside of work I've co-founded a parking management startup and enjoy contributing to open-source tools, exploring new backend technologies, and the occasional Conway's Game of Life implementation.
